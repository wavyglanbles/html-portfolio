Jon's Favourite Movies

My top 5 movies of all time.

Dragnet

Starring Dan Aykroyd and Tom Hanks

A straight-laced detective and his laid-back partner unravel a bizarre cult's plot in a comedic twist on classic crime procedurals.


Lost in Translation

Starring Bill Murray and Scarlett Johansson

Two strangers, adrift in a foreign city, form a deep connection while navigating loneliness and cultural disorientation.


The Fugitive

Starring Harrison Ford and Tommy Lee Jones

A wrongfully convicted doctor evades a relentless U.S. Marshal while uncovering the truth behind his wife's murder.


Cool Runnings

Starring John Candy

Against all odds, a Jamaican bobsled team captures the world's heart at the Winter Olympics.


Inside Out

Starring Amy Poehler

A young girl's emotions personified struggle to keep her balanced during a life-changing move to a new city.
